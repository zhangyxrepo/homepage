About Me

I am currently a research assistant at HKUST (Guangzhou), advised by Prof. Enyan Dai. I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I had the privilege of working with Prof. Xiaochun Ye. I obtained my B.S. degree from Xidian University.

My research interests include Graph Representation Learning, Graph Adversarial Attack, and Trustworthy AI.

Research Interests

My work focuses on graph representation learning, especially in the following directions:

  • Graph data mining and graph augmentation methods.

  • Graph adversarial attack and robustness of GNNs.

  • Reliable and trustworthy graph intelligence systems.

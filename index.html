About Me

I am currently a Research Assistant at HKUST (Guangzhou), advised by Prof. Enyan Dai. I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I was recommended for direct admission and worked with Prof. Xiaochun Ye. I obtained my B.S. degree from Xidian University.

My current research interests include Trustworthy AI, Graph Representation Learning, and VLA Safety.

Research Interests

My current research focuses on the following directions:

  • AI Safety: safety, robustness, and reliable evaluation of LLM, VLA and graph models.

  • Graph Representation Learning: graph data mining, graph robustness, and trustworthy graph intelligence.

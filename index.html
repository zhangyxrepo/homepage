About Me

I am a final-year graduate student(expected to graduate in 2025) at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, feeling fortunate to learn from Prof. Xiaochun Ye. Before that, I got my B.S degree at Xidian University and got recommended to manage for my M.S degree of Computer Science and Technology in ICT, CAS. My research interest includes Graph Representation Learning, Graph-based Adversarial Attack and Trust-worthy AI.
